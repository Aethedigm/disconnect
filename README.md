# disconnect
Ebitengine Game Jame 2026

Combat Controls :  
W/S : Forward/Reverse  
A/D : Turn  
Mouse : Aim  
Space : Shoot  
ESC : Pause/Unpause  

Menu Controls :  
Arrow Keys, Enter, ESC : Navigation  

Disconnect is a top down mecha battle game about capturing towers for communication and putting down enemy mecha!  

Choose your mecha loadout, then fight enemy mecha and capture towers, but be warned, the enemy are capturing back! Towers connect with overlapping Towers and Radio signals (commander units) and supply information to all connected units in the chain. The larger your network is, the better your friendly units strike force will be. Watch out though, the same goes for your enemies. If you step inside a red circle, the enemies in adjacent radio range will know you're there!  

Tips:  

Autoguns are the fastest firing by far, but do minimal damage. Most mecha have these. Rockets do substantial damage, but re-arming takes a long time. Snipers have range and power but are similar to Rockets in low speed.
